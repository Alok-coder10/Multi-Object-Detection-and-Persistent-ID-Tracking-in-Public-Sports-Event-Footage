YOLOv8 Detection

State-of-the-art anchor-free object detection. Detects all persons in each frame with configurable confidence thresholds.

⟲

ByteTrack Algorithm

Two-stage Hungarian assignment recovers low-confidence detections. Handles occlusion, blur, and crowded scenes.

△

Kalman Filter

Constant-velocity motion model predicts positions between detections. Persistent IDs survive up to 30 frames of occlusion.

◈

Movement Heatmap

Gaussian-blurred activity accumulator reveals player positioning patterns and high-traffic zones across the full video.

⊡

Trajectory Trails

Golden-ratio color-coded trails for each unique ID. Up to 60-frame history drawn with alpha-weighted thickness.

⊞

In-Browser Inference

ONNX Runtime Web runs detection directly in your browser. No server, no upload, no data leaves your device.
